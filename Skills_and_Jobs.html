Skills and Jobs



For the success of this project, the team has identified the need to employ qualified personnel that are specialised in these vital positions:

1. Architect
2. Mobile App Developer
3. Database Administrator
4. Marketing Manager

Architect

Job Description

The architect that Food Tunnelz is in search of should be able to perform all phases of architectural work such as the planning, designing and overseeing of the construction of Food Tunnelz with varying requirements dependant on location. The architect will be involved in the design of new Food Tunnelz, expansions, any alterations, restorations and conservations from the beginning to the end of construction.

Responsibilities

Oversee project from beginning to end.
Ensure high quality, innovative and functional design and construction, carried out to a specific high standard while strictly abiding to any building codes, guidelines and regulations.
Develop ideas according to the values of Food Tunnelz while minimising any environmental impact.
Produce detailed plans and alter any necessary corrections.
Ability to meet budget and timeline limits.
Make regular on-site visits to monitor status of project.
Cooperate and liaise with construction professionals.
Be up to date with architectural trends.

Requirements

Proven work experience of a minimum 7 years with a strong portfolio of successful projects to prove skill.
Excellent knowledge and competency in using design software such as Adobe Photoshop, Sketchup or similar.
Ability to be creative and imaginative.
High attention to detail.
Excellent skills in project management and teamwork.
Minimum of a bachelor's degree in Architecture.
Excellent knowledge of construction materials, techniques, and code.

Mobile App Developer

Job Description

The ideal candidate for the position of mobile app developer at Food Tunnelz should be able to manage both engineering and maintenance tasks. The primary responsibilities of this job entail the writing of code, implementing new user interfaces and features while ensuring a smooth application performance and a high standard of user experience.

Responsibilities

Write code at the highest quality in a clean, readable and organised manner.
Develop and implement new user interface and features.
Ensure a smooth application performance.
Ensure a high standard of user experience.
Fix any bugs and performance problems.

Requirements

Proven work experience of a minimum 3 years.
Excellent knowledge and competency in using programming software such as Android Studio, XCode and a good knowledge of Kotlin / Java, Swift.
Extensive knowledge of the Android and iOS operating systems.
Ability to be on call 24 hours in the case of any issues that require immediate attention.
Ability to work in a fast paced and highly dynamic environment.
Ability to be creative and imaginative.
High attention to detail.
Excellent skills in project management and teamwork.
Minimum of a bachelor's degree in:
Information Technology
Computer Science
Computer Software/Systems Engineering

Database Administrator

Job Description

This job entails the managing of the performance, integrity and security of a database, a vital component of Food Tunnelz. It involves the planning and development of a database, provisions for data security and recovery and the ongoing maintenance of consistent data.

Responsibilities

Efficient planning and development of database.
Develop requirements of users.
Monitor and maintain a high standard of data security.
Design the back-end data of the organisation and the front-end data accessible by the end user.
Install and test new database systems.
Ensure vital data recovery through developing, managing and testing any backup and recovery plans.
Control any access permission and user privileges.

Requirements

Proven work experience of a minimum 3 years.
Excellent knowledge and competency in using software such as Microsoft SQL Server 2016.
Ability to be on call 24 hours in the case of any issues that require immediate attention.
Highly organised and attentive to detail.
Excellent skills in project management and teamwork.
Minimum of a bachelor's degree in either:
Information Technology
Computer Science
Computer Software/Systems Engineering

Marketing Manager

Job Description

The position of marketing manager entails the managing of the marketing initiative of Food Tunnelz, through marketing research and analysis to the development and implementation of effective marketing strategy and planning. The position entails the overseeing of all marketing and promotional actions and campaigns. The marketing manager will report accurate marketing and sales results.

Responsibilities

Research and analyse the market and any competitors.
Create accurate market forecasts.
Establish marketing goals based on accurate forecasts.
Develop marketing strategies and plans.
Innovate effective and creative marketing and promotional campaigns.
Oversee any marketing and promotional actions.
Gain contracts with new cloud kitchens.
Track and report all marketing and sales data.

Requirements

Proven work experience of a minimum 3 years.
Excellent knowledge and competency in using marketing methods and principles.
Up to date with current marketing trends.
Excellent knowledge in social media platforms and their respective algorithms.
Ability to be creative and imaginative.
High attention to detail.
Ability to effectively communicate.
Excellent skills in project management and teamwork.
Minimum of a bachelor's degree in Marketing, Business or a related field.
CIM members will be desired.
